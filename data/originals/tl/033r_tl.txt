To make yellow dye
Take forth a shilling of
gamboge and [take] forth a
stuiver of yellow saffron and
a stuiver of alum and that
together, well broken up,
in water
Way to paint red
Take a quarter
brazilwood, a pint vinegar
and a half quarter alum
and let that seethe until [it reduces to]
the half, and then pour the soup out
in a flessche[glass/flask?]. That
keeps well for three years, and once
one wants to use it,
thus pour some of that soup in
an oyster shell and levigate
that 3 to 4 times [until] it [is] clear,
