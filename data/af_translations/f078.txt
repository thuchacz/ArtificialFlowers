Way to gild with gold or silver
The only thing that one wants to burnish is gold
or silver. One must first prepare the ground
with strong glue. Being dry, lay next
a layer of lime with white ceruse, paying attention
continually that you do not ever lay a second layer
unless the first is entirely dry. And do not
leave it ever to dry where it is very
sunny. And do not lay the white too thickly. One
must lay it 9 or 10 times. Then refine [it]
and make it very even with a

soap, and scour it with it until it
is even. Next, one must leave it to rest
at least one day. Before you go to gild or
silver it, only under the gild [areas] one puts
the two last coats of glue, some white
ceruse, and orange paint, on the ground
to have gold color. ~~~~
