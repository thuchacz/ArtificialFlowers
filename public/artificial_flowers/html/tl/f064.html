Blue dyed with brown, and pale starch blue, cambric is undyed with very hot water, the amount of a pint, one needs to change it in three or four hot waters, and one rinses it one time in cold water, next one pours all the water together stirring them ~ vigorously while pouring, in order that the blue will be ~ all amassed in one water, next one ~ leaves this water one hour, or two, without moving it, in order that the blue is all fallen ~ to the bottom, then one pours the water all gently taking care to [not] move the bottom, but at the end, when one sees that the blue comes with the water, before throwing it all away
