The cambric being quite dry, one polishes them with the hand, from both sides, and one folds them, each color inside a white paper. And when one wants to use them, one takes only one or two pieces of them at a time. ~~~~ Note: as I said before this, that the cords must be of horsehair in order to not attach, with the pins the cambrics to the cord.
