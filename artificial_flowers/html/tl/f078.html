Way to gild with gold or silver The only thing that one wants to burnish is gold or silver. One must first prepare the ground with strong glue. Being dry, lay next a layer of lime with white ceruse, paying attention continually that you do not ever lay a second layer unless the first is entirely dry. And do not leave it ever to dry where it is very sunny. And do not lay the white too thickly. One must lay it 9 or 10 times.
