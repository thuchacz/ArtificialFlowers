Then plunge the cambric immediately into the soup, until the soup becomes completely penetrated into the cambric but very well wet. One needs to open it well, and press, and turn it over, and rub it a bit, many times, so that the white becomes well penetrated into the cambric. From there, attach it to a cord with pins.~~ but if the cord is made of horse hair, it is enough to hang it, taking care that the end from which it hangs does not attach itself together. This command must be observed for all other colors. To make yellowish cloths for jasmine and other flowers, one needs to put with this starch white and fish glue three or four drops of gamboge To make some reddish ones One needs to take starch white, fish glue, and three or four drops of carmine. This kind of cambric is used to make carnations and other flowers Cloths of this sort can be green One makes them as above, by pouring a few drops from the bottle in which there is green. And if you want to have yellowish color, you put throughout the glue and the green one drop or two of gamboge. Blue cambric is made with starch brown-blue
