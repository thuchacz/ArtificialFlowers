Each piece of cambric or muslin must be washed piece by piece in the glue without taking more glue than that needed for the piece to be completely wet with the blue too. Then rub the blue very gently into the cambric, and when it is very blue, one needs to hold each one by the end in the hand and next pass the hand over it, from one end to the other, then back again, and so forth to make it very even. Next, you attach it or hang it by cord. But if the cambric is dry and it is not blue enough, one needs to plunge it again once more in the same soup, and do as above but without using starch white.
